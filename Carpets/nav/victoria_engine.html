Victoria - Engineered 14/3

The Victoria Engineered 14/3 collection is crafted to combine elegance with reliable performance. Featuring a 14mm board with a 3mm real wood wear layer, this flooring offers the authentic look and feel of hardwood while providing greater stability against changes in temperature and humidity. Easy to install and available in a variety of finishes, Victoria 14/3 is a versatile choice for creating warm, sophisticated interiors that are built to last.
